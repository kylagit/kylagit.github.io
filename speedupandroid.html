How to speed up a slow Android device

1. Uninstall/disable apps (Even built-in ones!) that you don't need

Most Android devices come with lots of built-in apps that take up storage, update themselves and send spam notifications without you even using them!

To uninstall/disable the apps you don't need, go to Settings > Apps > Choose an app that you don't need, then click Uninstall/disable and accept the warning

If you uninstalled, that was it!, if you diabled, you'll need to click the three dots in the top right corner then click "Uninstall updates" to free the storage space

Then restart your phone, you will notice a massive amount of storage freed, and significant improvements to speed and battery life
